\# Clinical Intel Chat MVP Scope



\## Purpose

Provide a free, open-source clinical intelligence chat for disease and therapy-area queries.



\## Inputs

\- Disease name

\- Disease alias

\- Therapy area

\- Drug name



\## Outputs

\- Overview

\- Symptoms

\- Diagnosis

\- ICD codes

\- Procedures if relevant

\- Treatments

\- Clinical trials

\- Literature

\- Sources

\- Disclaimer



\## Out of Scope

\- Medical advice

\- Patient-specific recommendations

\- SQL generation

\- Private data access

\- Autonomous actions

\- Long-term memory
